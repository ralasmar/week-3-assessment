Uptown Minneapolis

Get to Know my Neighborhood

I live in a subsection of Uptown Minneapolis known as the Wedge! One of my favorite things about living here is that it is a very walkable area. This makes it very easy to get to local restaurants, parks, stores, and more! Check out some of my favorite spots below!

Favorite Restaurants

  • Gyu Kaku

    • This Japanese BBQ restaurant is to die for! Best known for letting you cook the food right on the tabletop burners makes this not only a delicious choice but a great social activity!

  • Red Dragon

    • Hidden gem Chinese restaurant with delicious cocktails and even more delicious foods. Be sure to try the cream cheese wontons and dumplings!

  • Lynlake Brewery

    • A great spot for an evening with friends! With events like trivia, a large rooftop seating area, and delicious brews, this is a lovely spot for fun and a casual meal.

  • Lagos Tacos

    • A delicious choice for Mexican cuisine and margaritas! They even have a patio for those lucky days where it's warm enough to sit outside!

Favorite Local Activities

  • Lake of the Isles

    • I live a ten minute walk from the gorgeous Lake of the Isles! This provides for a great place to go for walks, bike rides, or even kayaking! It also makes for a great outdoor spot to sit and read or have a picnic with friends

  • Mueller Park

    • A lovely little gem of a local park with playground equipment for children, basketball courts, tennis courts, and picnic tables!

  • Up-Down Arcade

    • A great spot to grab drinks and play your favorite old-school arcade games!

  • The Cafe Meow

    • This is a cute spot where you can grab your favorite coffee or tea beverage and then spend time in a room full of friendly and adorable cats! If you're lucky you can spot some of the cats through the window as you walk by! And if you fall in love you can even adopt one!
